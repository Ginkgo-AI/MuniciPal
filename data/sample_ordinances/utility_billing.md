# Chapter 13.08 - UTILITY BILLING AND PAYMENT

## 13.08.010 - Billing Cycle

All municipal utility accounts (water, sewer, stormwater, and solid waste) are billed monthly. Statements are mailed or emailed on the 1st of each month and payment is due by the 20th. A late fee of $10.00 or 5% of the outstanding balance (whichever is greater) is applied after the due date.

## 13.08.020 - Payment Methods

Residents may pay their utility bill through any of the following methods:

1. **Online Portal** — Visit www.cityofexample.gov/pay to make a one-time payment or set up automatic payments using a bank account or credit/debit card. A convenience fee of $2.50 applies to card transactions.
2. **By Phone** — Call the Utility Billing Division at 555-0199 during business hours (Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM) to pay by phone.
3. **In Person** — Visit City Hall at 100 Main Street, 1st Floor, Finance Window. Cash, check, and card payments are accepted.
4. **Drop Box** — A 24-hour payment drop box is located at the main entrance of City Hall. Place check or money order (no cash) in a sealed envelope with your account number written on the outside.
5. **By Mail** — Send check or money order payable to "City of Example" to: Utility Billing, P.O. Box 1234, Example, VA 24011.

## 13.08.030 - AutoPay Enrollment

Residents may enroll in automatic payment through the online portal or by submitting an AutoPay Authorization Form to the Utility Billing Division. AutoPay drafts the full balance on the due date from the registered bank account. There is no fee for AutoPay.

## 13.08.040 - Disconnection for Non-Payment

Accounts that are 60 days past due are subject to service disconnection. A written notice is mailed 10 business days before disconnection. A reconnection fee of $75.00 applies. Residents experiencing financial hardship may contact the Utility Billing Division to discuss payment plan options.
